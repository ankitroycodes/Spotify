The Local Train

The Local Train is an Indian Hindi rock band known for blending rock, indie, and alternative music. Formed in 2008, they gained popularity for their soulful lyrics and powerful melodies. Their albums Aalas Ka Pedh and Vaaqif include hits like "Aaoge Tum Kabhi" and "Choo Lo." The band has built a strong fanbase through independent releases and live shows.
